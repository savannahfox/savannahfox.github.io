Welcome to Stumptown Pizzeria

With two locations in the Kerns and Sellwood-Moreland neighborhoods, we have been serving up artisanal pizza made with local and seasonal ingredients for the last twenty years! "Slow food" is our passion and we take pride in sourcing the finest organic fruits and vegetables for our food. Go ahead - try a slice!
